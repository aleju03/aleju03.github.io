# 3D model credits

Used in the AlejOS power-on scene and its house/yard roam mode
(`src/components/os/CrtScene.tsx`).

## CC BY 3.0 (attribution required)

Licensed CC BY 3.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).

- `computer.glb` is "Computer Generic 2000s" by Charlie (https://poly.pizza/m/P90iu7e4gY).
  Unmodified except for runtime material tweaks (screen glass and boot-text emissive).
- `keyboard.glb` is "Keyboard" by Poly by Google (https://poly.pizza/m/aPwr5p1dluw). Unmodified.
- `mouse.glb` is "Computer mouse" by Poly by Google (https://poly.pizza/m/6ikB7PIRVyd).
  Texture downscaled, geometry unmodified.
- `tree.glb` is "Tree" by Marc Solà (https://poly.pizza/m/6Yjt8nIwLsD). Geometry welded.
- `bench.glb` is "Park Bench" by J-Toastie (https://poly.pizza/m/kn0sMwbspb). Geometry welded.
